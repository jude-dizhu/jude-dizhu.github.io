Why I build this website?

I made this website for self-introduction.

Although I also prepared other materials, there are still things that cannot be covered by personal statement or CV. Through this website, I'd like to show a vivid picture about myself.

In this website, I will cover only campus life, as I have already introduced academic performances and career goals in other materials.

I am always open to new things. Although I did well in most courses, there are certain things that I learned outside the textbook. Until last summer, computer programming was totally new to me. I never thought that I would introduce myself through pieces of codes. I took a class online and learned to write some basic programs through Python, my first language. When my codes passed the test and the correct answer jumped into the screen, I started to feel the charm of coding. After that, I acquired the second language, HTML&CSS, through another online course. It feels so amazing that I am now building this website using this awesome language.
